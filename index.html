  • Wireframing

    Creating a visual representation of a website or application's layout and functionality, before the actual design and development begins.

    Wireframes are typically created using simple shapes and lines, with a focus on the placement of elements, such as buttons, forms, and navigation.

    • Help to establish the structure and layout of a website or application, before any visual design elements are added.
    • Allow designers and developers to experiment with different layouts and functionality.
    • Enable stakeholders to understand and provide feedback on the layout and functionality of a website or application.
    • Facilitate communication between designers, developers, and other stakeholders.
